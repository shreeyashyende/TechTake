# Support for TechTake
**Last Updated: January 19, 2026**

If you need help with TechTake, please contact our support team at shreeyashyende@gmail.com.

## How to get support
- **For technical issues or bugs:** email shreeyashyende@gmail.com with a description, device model, OS version, and steps to reproduce.
- **For privacy questions:** email shreeyashyende@gmail.com.
- **For feature requests:** create an issue in this repository and label it "feature request".

## FAQ

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: All data is stored locally on your device (SQLite and Shared Preferences). We do not collect or transmit personal data.

**Q: How can I delete my data?**
A: Uninstall the app or clear the app data from device settings.

**Q: Is the app offline?**
A: Yes, after installation the app works entirely offline and does not use third-party analytics.
