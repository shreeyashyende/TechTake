# Privacy Policy for TechTake

**Last Updated: January 18, 2026**

## Introduction

TechTake ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application TechTake ("the App").

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored Locally

TechTake stores all data locally on your device. We do **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. The following data is stored locally on your device:

- **Learning Progress**: Words you've mastered, words marked for revision, and your learning statistics
- **Preferences**: App settings and bookmarked words
- **Usage Statistics**: Daily streak count, XP points, and learning milestones

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics sent to external servers
- Any data shared with third parties

## Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using:
- SQLite database for learning progress and word data
- Shared Preferences for app settings

This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

## Third-Party Services

TechTake does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app functions entirely offline after installation.

### Text-to-Speech

The app uses your device's built-in text-to-speech functionality to pronounce words. This feature operates locally on your device and does not transmit any data externally.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security settings. We recommend:
- Using a device passcode or biometric lock
- Keeping your device's operating system updated
- Not jailbreaking or rooting your device

## Children's Privacy

TechTake does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:
- Uninstalling the app from your device
- Clearing the app's data through your device settings

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

**Email**: techtakeapp@gmail.com

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete from our systems. All your data remains under your control on your device.
